Oranges

Citrus and Bright

Oranges are refreshing, juicy, and vibrant. Like a lot of other fruits, they have a shell that either gets pealed or avoided when eating. Some people will eat the rind. I like to eat oranges on a hot summer day, or to just amplify vitamins in my body.
